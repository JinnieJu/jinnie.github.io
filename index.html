Hello! I am a PhD student at the Department of Atmospheric Sciences, Kyungpook National University, just started in September 2023.

I am interested in tropical cyclone. I studied long-term change of tropical cyclone activity around East Asia during MS. During my PhD, I would like to learn more about the mesoscale dynamics of tropical cyclone such as the processes of development, and dissipation as well as structures.

Research interests:

  • Mesoscale dynamics of tropical cyclones
  • Climatological characteristics of tropical cyclones
  • Remote sensing of tropical cyclones
